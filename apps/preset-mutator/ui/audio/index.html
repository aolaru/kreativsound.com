Input Audio

Upload one short source sound and generate playable Vital preset starts inspired by its tone and shape.

Best inputs: one-shot notes, basses, pads, plucks, drones, and clean textures. Supported formats: WAV, MP3, AIFF, M4A, OGG, FLAC. Max 10 MB.

Avoid full songs, long loops, and dense mixes. They usually produce weaker mappings.
