<p>Kreativ Sound is focusing the browser tool area around <strong>Preset Mutator</strong>: one local-first app for creating Vital presets from scratch, from an existing preset, or from audio.</p>

<p>The default path is now <strong>From Scratch</strong>: choose a musical intent, shape the direction, and generate Vital preset starts without needing a source file.</p>

<p>Vital is the first supported format. Serum 2 and Arturia Pigments 7 are planned as the next synth targets, so Preset Mutator can grow without another product rename.</p>

<p>This keeps the promise narrow and practical: one tool, three clear creation paths, and no separate unfinished suite messaging.</p>

<h2>What is live now</h2>
<p><strong>From Scratch</strong>, <strong>Mutate Preset</strong>, and <strong>Audio to Preset</strong> are available today as Vital beta workflows. The main app opens on From Scratch because it is the lowest-friction starting point.</p>

<h2>What comes next</h2>
<p>Serum 2 and Pigments 7 support remain planned alongside the next format expansion after the current Vital workflow is solid.</p>

<h2>Why this direction</h2>
<p>Preset Mutator fits the current Kreativ Sound catalog: practical, local-first, fast enough for sound-design sketching, and focused on usable starting points rather than heavy studio software.</p>
