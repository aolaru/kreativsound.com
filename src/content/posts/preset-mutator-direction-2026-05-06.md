<p>Kreativ Sound is refocusing the browser tool area around <strong>Preset Mutator</strong> as the main public tool product.</p>

<p>The first live path is <strong>Audio to Preset</strong>: upload one short source sound, analyze it locally in the browser, and generate Vital preset directions that can be refined further inside the synth.</p>

<p>Vital is the first supported format. Serum 2 and Arturia Pigments 7 are planned as the next synth targets, so Preset Mutator can grow into a broader preset-generation workflow without changing product names again.</p>

<p>This keeps the promise narrow and practical. The goal is not a large unfinished suite. The goal is one tool with clear creation paths, released only when each path is strong enough to be useful.</p>

<h2>What is live now</h2>
<p>Audio to Preset for Vital is available today. It works best with short pads, plucks, bass notes, drones, and textures instead of full songs or dense mixes.</p>

<h2>What comes next</h2>
<p><strong>Mutate Preset</strong> is now available as a Vital beta inside Preset Mutator. <strong>From Scratch</strong> remains planned as a future mode, with Serum 2 and Pigments 7 support planned alongside the next format expansion after the current Vital workflow is solid.</p>

<h2>Why this direction</h2>
<p>Preset Mutator fits the current Kreativ Sound catalog: practical, local-first, fast enough for sound-design sketching, and focused on usable starting points rather than heavy studio software.</p>
