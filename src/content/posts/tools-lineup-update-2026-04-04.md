<p>The Kreativ Sound tools area is being refocused around Preset Mutator as the main browser tool product.</p>
        <p>From Scratch, Mutate Preset, and Audio to Preset now live inside the same Preset Mutator app instead of separate public products.</p>
        <p>The current goal is to keep them lightweight, local-first, and practical enough for fast sound-design workflows rather than heavy studio software.</p>
