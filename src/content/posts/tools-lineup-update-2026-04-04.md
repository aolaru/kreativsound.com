<p>The Kreativ Sound tools area now includes three public browser tools: Audio Alchemy, Preset Mutator, and Wave Fracture.</p>
        <p>Each tool is built around a clear transformation path. Audio Alchemy turns audio into preset ideas, Preset Mutator turns one preset into new preset directions, and Wave Fracture turns one sound into fractured audio variations.</p>
        <p>The current goal is to keep them lightweight, local-first, and practical enough for fast sound-design workflows rather than heavy studio software.</p>
