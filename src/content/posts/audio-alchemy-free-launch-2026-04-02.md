<p>Preset Mutator is now live on Kreativ Sound with Audio to Preset mode: a browser-based workflow for turning short source audio into playable Vital presets.</p>
        <p>Vital is the first supported format. Serum 2 and Arturia Pigments 7 are planned as future synth targets once the current workflow is stable.</p>
        <p>The first free version focuses on a simple flow: upload one short sound, analyze it locally in the browser, then generate three preset variations you can download and use in your own productions.</p>
        <p>Nothing is sent to AI models, nothing is uploaded to a server, and the current version is built to feel fast enough for quick sound-design experiments rather than heavy processing sessions.</p>
        <p>The best results usually come from short, simple material such as pads, plucks, bass notes, drones, and textures instead of full songs or dense mixes.</p>
