Tools Lineup Update

Published April 4, 2026

The Kreativ Sound tools area now includes three public browser tools: Audio Alchemy, Preset Mutator, and Wave Fracture.

Each tool is built around a clear transformation path. Audio Alchemy turns audio into preset ideas, Preset Mutator turns one preset into new preset directions, and Wave Fracture turns one sound into fractured audio variations.

The current goal is to keep them lightweight, local-first, and practical enough for fast sound-design workflows rather than heavy studio software.
