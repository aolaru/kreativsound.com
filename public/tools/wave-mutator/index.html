Beta tool

Drop in messy WAV files. Export cleaner samples and pack previews.

Wave Mutator is built for preparing sample-pack material, preset-source audio, one-shots, loops, noise layers, and sound-product demos. It trims silence, adds short fades, normalizes peak level, warns about clipping, exports cleaned WAV files, and can create a short MP3 preview montage when your browser supports local MP3 recording.

For Sound designers, musicians, preset creators, sample-pack makers.
Privacy Files are decoded, processed, and exported locally in the browser.
Formats WAV input. WAV/ZIP export. MP3 montage depends on browser support.
Beta limits No LUFS normalization, spectral editing, AI tagging, or full DAW-style repair yet.
