Creative tools for reshaping sound.

A focused set of browser tools for turning audio into presets, mutating presets into new directions, and breaking sounds into new audio textures.

Audio -> Preset

Audio Alchemy

Upload a source sound and generate three free synth preset variants inspired by it. Vital is supported now, with more synths planned in the future.

Best for short pads, plucks, basses, drones, and textures.

Open Audio Alchemy
Preset -> Preset

Preset Mutator

Load one Vital preset, bias the mutation, and export three new preset directions without leaving the browser.

Best for pushing one strong preset into subtle, bolder, and wilder variants.

Open Preset Mutator
Audio -> Audio

Wave Fracture

Upload one short sound, generate three fractured audio variations, preview them instantly, and export local WAVs.

Best for impacts, drones, one-shots, and textures that need more breakage and motion.

Open Wave Fracture
